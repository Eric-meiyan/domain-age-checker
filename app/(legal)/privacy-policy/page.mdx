# Privacy Policy for AIDomain

## Introduction

Welcome to AIDomain, the **AI-Powered Domain Search Platform** designed to help you find perfect domains through bulk search and intelligent keyword generation. At AIDomain, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our domain search services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using AIDomain:

1. **Search Query Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Domain search keywords, AI prompts you submit, selected TLD preferences, and search results.
   - **Purpose**: To provide domain availability checking, improve AI keyword generation, and enhance search accuracy.

2. **Usage Analytics**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use AIDomain, including search frequency, feature usage (AI vs manual mode), and interaction patterns.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our AI algorithms, and optimize the domain search experience.

3. **Device and Technical Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access AIDomain, such as device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices, ensure compatibility, and provide security protection.

4. **Cookies and Local Storage**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us remember your preferences, search history, and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our domain search tools and personalize your search experience.

5. **Account Information (Optional)**
   - **What We Collect**: If you choose to create an account, this includes your email address and any profile information you provide.
   - **Purpose**: To save your search preferences, provide search history, and offer personalized recommendations.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.

## AI Data Processing

When you use our AI-powered keyword generation feature:

- **AI Prompts**: Your input prompts are processed by AI services to generate relevant domain keywords.
- **Data Retention**: AI prompts and generated keywords may be temporarily stored to improve our AI models and service quality.
- **Third-Party AI Services**: We may use third-party AI providers to process your requests. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
- **No Personal Identification**: AI processing does not link your prompts to personal identifying information unless you explicitly provide it.

## Changes to This Policy

You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
